Visit Sydney

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of CBD.

Top three activities to do at Sydney

Go for a picnic in the local park

Bicentennial Park is perfect for picnic, getting some sun or just chilling out.

Attend a show or a concert

Enmore Theatre is a great place to spend your night out.

Get sweaty at the sports field

Olympic Park's Sports Hall, you can play Volleyball, Football, Basketball or lift some weights.
